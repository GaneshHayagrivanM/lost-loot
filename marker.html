🎯 Hiro Marker

Use this marker with your AR app!

📋 How to use:

  1. Print this marker on paper OR display it on another screen
  2. Go back to the AR app
  3. Point your camera at this marker
  4. Watch the 3D box appear!

💡 Tips:

  • Make sure the marker is well-lit
  • Keep the marker flat and visible
  • The marker should be about 5-10cm in size for best results
  • Hold your device steady for better tracking
